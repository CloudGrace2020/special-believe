A journey of a thousand steps

In the top 10 of list is on number one, climbing Mount Kilimanjaro in Tanzania, Africa. So in late 2015 i decided to put myself up for an adventure. The expedition to "Kili" started with a well trained period of six months preparing myself, physically and mentally for the journey.
